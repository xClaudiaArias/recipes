Caramel

Ingredients

1/2 cup of each

  • Butter
  • Corn Syrup
  • Brown Sugar
  • Sugar
  • Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • 1 tspn Vanilla

How To Cook

  1. Butter your square pan.
  2. Melt butter in a separate bowl.
  3. Stir in corn syrup, both sugars and sweetened condensed milk until most of the sugar is dissolved.
  4. Microwave on high for 3 minutes and 5-15 seconds.
  5. Remove from the microwave and stir it.
  6. Microwave again for the same time and stir in the vanilla.
  7. Pour immidiately into the buttered bowl.
  8. Let it sit and cool for several hours then cut into squares.

ENJOY!
