Cocoa Milk Truffles

Ingredients

  • Condensed Milk
  • Cocoa Powder

How To Cook

Step 1: Prepare the chocolate truffle dough

  1. Line your mold with wax paper
  2. Pour condensed milk into a bowl. Make sure it is not too thick.
  3. Gradually sift in the cocoa powder and stir in between until it completely incorporated.
  4. When the mixture thickens, lightly oil your hands and knead the dough by hand until it is smooth. When it's done, it should not be ticky.

If it is too sticky, add a little bit more the cocoa powder. if it's too dry, add a bit more of the condensed milk.

Step 2: Chill and slice

  1. Refrigerate the dough for 1 hour.
  2. Once its chilled, pull it out of the wax paper and transfer it to a cutting board.
  3. Sift some cocoa powder on top of the truffle dough, then cut it into squares with a sharp knife.
  4. Optional: round the corners with your fingers.

ENJOY!
