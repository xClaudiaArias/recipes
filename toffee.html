Toffee

Ingredients

  • 2 cups butter
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1/4 tspn salt
  • 1 tspn vanilla
  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped almonds

How To Cook

  1. Cover a large baking sheet with the aluminum foil or parchment paper.
  2. In a medium large heavy bottomed saucepan, combine the butter, sugar and salt over low heat until butter is melted, whisking constantly. Bring to a steady boil over medium to high heat and whisk.
  3. Remove toffee from heat and sir in vanilla. Pour mixture into baking sheet.
  4. Sprinkle the chocolate chips over the top and let the chocolate soften for a few minutes. Spread the chocolate into a thin layer.
  5. Sprinkle the chopped almonds.
  6. Refrigerate until set and break into smaller pieces.

ENJOY!
